Running

I first started running competitively in the 6th grade for my middle school track and field team and ever since then I have only grown to enjoy running more! I have enjoyed both the physical benefits of running along with the relationships that I have made with fellow runners. Long distance running has taught me the value of perseverence and the value of giving 100% of your energy to achieving a goal. You can see the reults of my latest marathon (Richmond) here by searching the name 'Scott McGowan'. I will also be running the Richmond Marathon again on November 16, 2019!

Cubing

I have been competitively speedcubing since the fall of 2016. Since then, I have amassed a collection of over 50 geometric puzzles and have improved at speedsolving the Rubiks's cube. I have met many friends through cubing and have gotten the opportunity to travel all over the east coast to compete in official World Cube Association competitions. I also was a founding member and am the current president of the Rubik's Cubing at Virginia Tech club.
